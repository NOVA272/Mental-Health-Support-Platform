Mental Health Quiz

Identify Potential Concerns

Welcome to the Mental Health Quiz

Answer the following questions to the best of your ability. This quiz is designed to help identify potential mental health concerns. Please note that this is not a diagnostic tool and should not replace professional advice.

1. How often do you feel sad or depressed?

2. Do you experience excessive worry or anxiety?

3. How often do you have trouble sleeping?

4. Do you experience mood swings?

5. How often do you feel overwhelmed by stress?

6. Do you have difficulty concentrating?

7. How often do you feel irritable or angry?

8. Do you experience panic attacks?

9. How often do you feel disconnected from reality?

10. Do you have intrusive thoughts that you can't control?

11. How often do you feel hopeless or worthless?

12. Do you avoid social situations due to fear or anxiety?

13. How often do you feel excessively tired or fatigued?

14. Do you have difficulty controlling your impulses?

15. How often do you feel detached from your surroundings?
